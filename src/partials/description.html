How to Play

  • 1

    Start each level by reading the question or instruction

  • 2

    Interact with objects on the screen by tapping, swiping, or shaking your device

  • 3

    Think creatively to solve puzzles—answers are rarely straightforward

  • 4

    Use hints if you get stuck on a particularly tricky level

  • 5

    Levels can include visual puzzles, word play, and logic problems

  • 6

    Expect the unexpected; solutions may involve unconventional methods

  • 7

    Progress through the game to unlock more complex challenges

  • 8

    No penalties for wrong answers—experiment freely
