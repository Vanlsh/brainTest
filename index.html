About the Game

"Brain Test Premium" is an enhanced version of the popular "Brain Test: Tricky Puzzles" game. It offers a variety of challenging riddles and brain teasers designed to test logic and creativity without the distraction of ads. The game features intuitive mechanics wrapped in a visually engaging interface, where each level presents unique problems that can often have unexpected solutions, promoting outside-the-box thinking.
